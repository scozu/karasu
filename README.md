## Overview

Karasu combines the stark contrasts and deep blacks of Black Metal aesthetics with the proven color relationships of Gruvbox Material Dark, the sophisticated palette of Kanagawa Dragon, and the subtle elegance of Kanso Pearl.

## Design Philosophy

- **Deep, atmospheric blacks** - Multiple levels of dark backgrounds for depth
- **Muted, metallic accents** - Low saturation colors with subtle metallic tones
- **High contrast** - Readable while maintaining atmospheric darkness
- **Consistent experience** - Unified palette across all supported platforms
